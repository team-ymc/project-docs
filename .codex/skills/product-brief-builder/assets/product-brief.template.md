# Product Brief

## 1. Product Definition

Describe what the product is in one or two sentences.

## 2. Product Purpose

Explain why the product exists and what user problem it is meant to solve.

## 3. Intended Users

Describe who the product is designed for.

Also mention important user groups the product is not designed around yet.

## 4. Core Experience

Describe the essential experience the product should provide to users.

Focus on the user-facing experience, not implementation details.

## 5. Current Capabilities

List the major capabilities the product currently supports or is actively
expected to support in the current product shape.

These should be product capabilities, not API endpoints or technical tasks.

## 6. Future Opportunities

List major opportunities the product may expand toward later.

These are not commitments for the current build.

## 7. Product Constraints

List stable product or architecture constraints that should guide decisions.

These should help future frontend, backend, and AI-server work stay aligned.
